A Rapid-Growth Investment Company For Competitive Professionals

An Investment Team Founded On Core Principles

Think Huge Investments was founded with one goal in mind; to provide you with an alternative and highly profitable investment vehicle.

Our vastly experienced traders capitalize on inefficiencies in the global currency market (Forex) to frequently capture profitable opportunities. Over time the accumulation of all this trades transacted results in our ability to outperform most other investment vehicles. We have the potential to aggressively grow your wealth rapidly.

Having said that, when you invest with Think Huge Investments you can be assured that the stability of your investment is one of our primary concerns. Your funds managed by experienced traders with their personal capital at stake

Every single trader on the team at Think Huge Investments has significant personal capital at stake in addition to the funds under management. All of the traders we approve of have been trading professionally for 5+ years and it’s our unique requirements that ensure our traders remain vigilant and committed to growing your funds over time.

Our traders capitalise on currency movements within the world’s leading economies. Often opportunities last all but a few seconds and only the most skilled and talented individuals are able to identify, capture and convert the opportunities consistently over the long term.

Every one of our traders has been through a rigorous vetting process (which can take up to 12 months to complete) to ensure that all client funds are managed with the utmost care and skill.

This has been a key factor into why we’re able to achieve consistent and above market returns since our inception.

Industry leading transparency: Know what is happening with your investment at all times

Every trader features a Myfxbook verified track record to give you comfort that we are who we say we are.

You can view the status of your investment 24hrs per day through your smartphone and actually watch positions being managed live in the market. This is in addition to the daily activity statements that features all the trades that have been placed on your account in the previous 24hrs. This is emailed automatically to you at the end of each day.

Regulated, Think Huge Investments complies with Australian regulation across all our managed discretionary accounts

Investor funds are separately held in trust under the client’s name at all times through our executing broker. We don’t have access to your funds without permission and you remain in complete control of your investment at all times.
