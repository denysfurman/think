Steady Capture Strategy

Forex Managed Discretionary Account (MDA/MAM)

Steady Capture is controlled by a manual trader from Canada with over 10 years experience in generating returns from the markets.

He uses multiple time frames to identify high probability opportunities across all the major currency pairs as well as Gold and Silver. A variety of strategies are used to capitalise on opportunities from scalping trades that last a few seconds to swing trades targeting hundreds of pips that could last as long as a week.

Since inception our clients have enjoyed above market returns across an array of market conditions. This is due to Steady Captures unique ability to harness the an arsenal of proprietary filters and tools to execute the right strategies for the volatility on offer.

As part of our mission to deliver industry-beating returns and aligning our interests with those of our clients, we charge no set-up fees, no ongoing management fees and no exit fees. Clients only pay a success fee on profits generated on their accounts and are free to withdraw their investment at any time.
